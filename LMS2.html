Welcome to the Libary Management System!





Here in our libary, we have over a thousand books for every one of your needs. Say you need a textbook for that difficult math class. We got it here in the shelves! Need a source that is not available in the bookstore anymore? Come over an talk to one of our experienced librarians to help you find what you are looking for from the reference area. We have all the finest works of literature from A to Z. From the oldest texts to fresh off the printing press.

Who Are We?

For over 85 years, we have been providing service to the students, staff, and neighbours around Casablanca University. Our staff has dedicated themselves to providing the most up-to-date books as well as preserving several pieces of literary work for years to come. Our mission is to serve the entire community by allowing customers and the school body to borrow our books for their academic use or just for their enterainment. We expect those who use our properites to treat them with care as we do not hesitate to deal with late or damaged goods. As we strive to take care of our literary works, we encourage our school as well as those outside of it to do the same. Feel free to learn or just have a space of peace and quiet as we continue to deliver the best values in books and update our stock with brand new novels, biography and everything related to literature. We are the 'brain' of this campus. And we are here to deliver the knowledge needed for you to succeed.
